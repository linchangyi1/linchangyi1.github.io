Accelerator

Ingeniously apply the meshing between double-sided synchronous belt and connecting plate to convert the accurate circular motion of motors into linear motion. It firstly store energy in the high-inertial subsystem and reach a high but stable speed, and then send the plate with a rack accommodating a glider to mesh with the outer teeth of the belt.

Decelerate and Deadlock Mechanism

The rack attaching to the connecting plate is decelerated with dampening springs and locked with a rachet mechanism once the drone is ejected out; rack is finally released with a linear actuator unlocking mechanism.

Reloading Mechanism

A curved-rail-guided system that relocates drones onto launching rack using one single actuator.

DIY designed Yaw Bearing

Self-designed and manufactured a ring bearing for launcher’s yaw axis. Minimizes backlash and off-axis movements while reducing overall system cost compared to industrial counterparts.

Rebar Processing Euqipment

The rebar processing euqipment consisting of four moudles is used to process volumed rebar into retiform rebar.

Four moudles: 

  • NO.1 Straightening mechanism.

  • NO.2 Cutter mechanism.

  • NO.3 Transfering mechanism.

  • NO.4 Welding mechanism.

Straightening Mechanism

Because rebar is usually tied up in a roll, firstly we need to straighten the rebar. the rebar will accross the space between the up two idler wheels and the down three ones. And the gap between them can be justified by the motor to fit different sizes of rebar.

Cutter Mechanism

We use cutter mechanism to cut the straight rebar into ones in specific length.

Transfering Mechanism

After every straight rebar fall into the groove in the roller, we need to strongly hold it until it's transfered to the welding plate below the device. Therefore I designed a mechanical arm which can hold heavy rebar with only a mini-driver.

Welding Mechanism

The 2D welding device is used to shape the retiform rebar and move it to be welded by a welding arm.
